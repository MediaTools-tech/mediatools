About MediaTools Video Transcoder

A comprehensive solution for all your video transcoding needs

MediaTools Video Transcoder is a powerful desktop application built around the renowned FFmpeg engine, providing you with the ability to convert videos between a wide variety of formats. Whether you're looking to prepare videos for different devices, reduce file sizes, or change codecs, our application makes the process simple and efficient.

Quick Start Command - Docker Hub:

E:\>mkdir test
E:\>mkdir test\uploads
E:\>mkdir test\temp
E:\>mkdir test\outputs

docker run -d -p 8080:8080 \
  -v E:/test/:/app/storage \
  baladockerbytes/video-transcoder:latest

After running, open http://localhost:8080 in your browser.
