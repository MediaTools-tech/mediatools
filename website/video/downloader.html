About MediaTools Video Downloader

A comprehensive solution for all your video downloading needs

MediaTools Video Downloader is a powerful desktop application built around the renowned yt-dlp engine, providing you with the ability to download videos from over 1000 websites. Whether you're looking to save educational content, memorable moments, or entertainment for offline viewing, our application makes the process simple and efficient.

Built on yt-dlp

Our application leverages the powerful yt-dlp project, an open-source YouTube-dl fork with additional features and improvements. This ensures maximum compatibility with video platforms and regular updates to handle changes in website structures.

The application features an intuitive user interface that makes video downloading accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise. Simply paste the video URL, choose your preferred quality and format, and let the application handle the rest.

All downloads are performed with respect to platform terms of service, and we encourage users to only download content they have the right to access offline.
