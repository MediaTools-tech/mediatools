About MediaTools Video Downloader v2.1.0

A comprehensive solution for all your video and audio downloading needs

MediaTools Video Downloader v2.1.0 is a powerful desktop application built around the renowned yt-dlp engine, with integrated spotdl, providing you with the ability to download videos and audio from over 1000 websites. Whether you're looking to save educational content, memorable moments, music, or entertainment for offline viewing, our application makes the process simple and efficient.

Built on yt-dlp & spotdl. Enhanced Format Support in v2.1.0

Now with expanded audio extraction capabilities from both video and audio platofrms! Download videos in multiple formats (MKV for quality preservation, MP4 for compatibility) or extract audio in popular formats like M4A and MP3. Perfect for creating music libraries or saving podcast episodes from ytdlp/spotdl supported platoforms.

The application features an intuitive user interface that makes video and audio downloading accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise. Simply paste the video URL, choose your preferred quality and format (video or audio), and let the application handle the rest.
